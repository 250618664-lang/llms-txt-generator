About AI Search Visibility Scores

AI search visibility is an estimate of how likely your website is to be referenced in AI-generated responses. Unlike traditional SEO scores, it depends on both technical setup and content authority.

  • Technical setup — llms.txt, robots.txt, sitemap.xml, structured data, and crawler policies are the foundational elements.
  • Content accessibility — AI systems can only reference content they can access. Paywalled or JavaScript-heavy content is harder to use.
  • Content authority — Backlinks, citations, and domain authority still matter in AI search — AI systems prefer authoritative sources.
⚠️ This is an estimate. The score reflects your self-reported inputs. It does not guarantee AI citations, search rankings, or visibility. AI search results depend on content quality and relevance, not just technical setup.
