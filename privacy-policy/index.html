Introduction

WebToolkitAI ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website webtoolkitai.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our free online tools.

By using WebToolkitAI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

What We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Tool usage data (anonymous) — When you use our tools, we may log which tools are used, which buttons are clicked, and general session information. This data is aggregated and anonymous and is used to understand which tools are useful.
  • Analytics data — We use Google Analytics 4 to collect standard web analytics data including page views, referring URLs, browser type, and general geographic information (country/region level only).
  • User inputs — Any text, URLs, or data you enter into our tools are processed in your browser only. This data is never sent to our servers, stored on our servers, or shared with any third parties.

Google Analytics 4

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site. GA4 collects standard information such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or direct traffic
  • Browser and device type
  • Country and region (general geographic location)

GA4 uses cookies to distinguish unique visitors. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Our GA4 Measurement ID is G-1E4EP66Y0Q.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Google Analytics — standard analytics cookies to distinguish unique visitors
  • No authentication cookies — we do not have login or user accounts, so we do not use authentication cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses (unless voluntarily submitted through our contact form).
  • We do not store, log, or transmit the data you enter into our tools to any server.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any collected data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We do not use the data you enter into our tools for any purpose — it stays in your browser.
